Who We Are

We are a team of college students interested in how data, AI, and clear communication can support everyday businesses. Our project started with a simple question: why are so many donuts and cups of coffee thrown away at the end of the day at a campus Dunkin’ if we supposedly “know” what demand will look like?

That led us to research predictive analytics and create this website as a guide for small business owners and students like us who want a practical, non‑technical introduction.

Our Goals

  • Explain predictive analytics using clear language and real examples.
  • Show how small datasets (like one store’s sales) can still be useful.
  • Offer simple starting points that do not require programming experience.
